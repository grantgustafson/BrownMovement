Regulars Rating for Major Access Point Areas

This graph expresses the Regulars Rating for 8 key access points around campus. The regulars rating expresses the number of repeated users per connection area on campus. In essence, it shows how many people visit each access point more than once in our dataset. The Regulars Rating is calculated using the formula: (Total # of Connections - # of Distinct Users) / (Total # of Connections).
Each point on the graph expresses the average connection time and regulars rating for each access point. The each line represents the max and min for connection time and regulars rating for each access point.
